About Viacodos
Via Codos is a forward-thinking IT solutions provider dedicated to solving real-world problems through technology. Founded in March under the Trading Places Registration Statute 4 (Southern Provincial) 2019, we specialize in web and mobile app development, cloud services, security systems, and tailored software solutions. Though it is a single-owner business, Via Codos thrives through the collaborative efforts of a dynamic and skilled team of undergraduates from SLIIT, the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, and Pakistani universities.
Our mission is to deliver simple solutions for simple problems while strategically reducing complexity for complex challenges. With a vision of building virtual solutions for a better future, our team upholds ethics and innovation in every project we undertake.
